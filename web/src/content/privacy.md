# Privacy

*Last updated: placeholder*

## What we collect

When you create an account, Scholia stores your email, display name, and the
content you produce on the site (notes, quotations, articles). That's it.

## What we don't do

- We do not sell your data.
- We do not run third-party ad trackers.
- We do not share what you've read or annotated with anyone else.

## Cookies

Scholia uses cookies only for keeping you signed in.

## Contact

Questions about your data can be sent to the project maintainer.
